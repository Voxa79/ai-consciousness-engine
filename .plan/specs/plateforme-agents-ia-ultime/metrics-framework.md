## 2. MÉTRIQUES BUSINESS - GROWTH INTELLIGENCE

### 2.1 Métriques Revenue & Growth
```yaml
Revenue Metrics:
  
  Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):
    - Définition: Revenue récurrent mensuel
    - Mesure: Sum(monthly subscriptions)
    - Target: 20% MoM growth
    - Fréquence: Daily tracking
    - Action: <15% growth = growth strategy review
    
  Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):
    - Définition: MRR x 12 (forward-looking)
    - Mesure: Committed annual revenue
    - Target: $1M Year 1, $10M Year 2
    - Fréquence: Monthly calculation
    - Action: Behind target = sales acceleration
    
  Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):
    - Définition: Revenue moyen par customer
    - Mesure: Total revenue / active customers
    - Target: $500/month (premium positioning)
    - Fréquence: Monthly analysis
    - Action: <$400 = pricing optimization
    
  Revenue Growth Rate:
    - Définition: Croissance revenue month-over-month
    - Mesure: (Current MRR - Previous MRR) / Previous MRR
    - Target: >20% MoM (hypergrowth)
    - Fréquence: Monthly tracking
    - Action: <15% = growth initiatives required
```

### 2.2 Métriques Customer Acquisition
```yaml
Acquisition Metrics:
  
  Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):
    - Définition: Coût acquisition nouveau customer
    - Mesure: Sales & Marketing spend / New customers
    - Target: <$500 (efficient acquisition)
    - Fréquence: Monthly calculation
    - Action: >$750 = acquisition optimization
    
  CAC Payback Period:
    - Définition: Temps récupération CAC
    - Mesure: CAC / (ARPU x Gross Margin)
    - Target: <6 months (fast payback)
    - Fréquence: Monthly analysis
    - Action: >9 months = unit economics review
    
  Lead Conversion Rate:
    - Définition: % leads convertis en customers
    - Mesure: Paying customers / Total leads
    - Target: >15% (high-quality leads)
    - Fréquence: Weekly tracking
    - Action: <10% = sales process optimization
    
  Viral Coefficient:
    - Définition: Nombre referrals par customer
    - Mesure: New customers from referrals / Total customers
    - Target: >0.5 (organic growth)
    - Fréquence: Monthly calculation
    - Action: <0.3 = referral program enhancement
```

### 2.3 Métriques Customer Success
```yaml
Success Metrics:
  
  Net Promoter Score (NPS):
    - Définition: Satisfaction et recommandation client
    - Mesure: % Promoters - % Detractors
    - Target: >70 (world-class satisfaction)
    - Fréquence: Monthly survey
    - Action: <50 = customer experience improvement
    
  Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):
    - Définition: Satisfaction interaction-level
    - Mesure: % satisfied responses (4-5/5)
    - Target: >90% (exceptional experience)
    - Fréquence: Per interaction
    - Action: <85% = product quality review
    
  Customer Retention Rate:
    - Définition: % customers retained month-over-month
    - Mesure: (Customers end - New customers) / Customers start
    - Target: >95% (sticky product)
    - Fréquence: Monthly calculation
    - Action: <90% = churn analysis required
    
  Net Revenue Retention (NRR):
    - Définition: Revenue retention + expansion
    - Mesure: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting MRR
    - Target: >120% (negative churn)
    - Fréquence: Monthly tracking
    - Action: <110% = expansion strategy review
```

## 3. MÉTRIQUES OPÉRATIONNELLES - EFFICIENCY INTELLIGENCE

### 3.1 Métriques Team & Productivity
```yaml
Team Metrics:
  
  Revenue Per Employee:
    - Définition: Efficiency revenue par team member
    - Mesure: ARR / Total employees
    - Target: >$200K (high productivity)
    - Fréquence: Quarterly analysis
    - Action: <$150K = productivity optimization
    
  Engineering Velocity:
    - Définition: Story points delivered per sprint
    - Mesure: Completed story points / Sprint
    - Target: 20% improvement quarterly
    - Fréquence: Per sprint (2 weeks)
    - Action: Declining velocity = process review
    
  Code Quality Score:
    - Définition: Technical debt et quality metrics
    - Mesure: SonarQube composite score
    - Target: >90% (maintainable codebase)
    - Fréquence: Per commit
    - Action: <85% = code quality initiative
    
  Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS):
    - Définition: Satisfaction et engagement team
    - Mesure: % Promoters - % Detractors (employees)
    - Target: >50 (engaged workforce)
    - Fréquence: Quarterly survey
    - Action: <30 = culture improvement program
```

### 3.2 Métriques Financial Health
```yaml
Financial Metrics:
  
  Gross Margin:
    - Définition: Profitabilité après coûts directs
    - Mesure: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
    - Target: >80% (software economics)
    - Fréquence: Monthly calculation
    - Action: <75% = cost structure optimization
    
  Burn Rate:
    - Définition: Cash consumption mensuelle
    - Mesure: (Cash start month - Cash end month)
    - Target: <$100K/month (efficient operations)
    - Fréquence: Weekly monitoring
    - Action: >$150K = expense review required
    
  Runway:
    - Définition: Mois restants avant cash depletion
    - Mesure: Current cash / Monthly burn rate
    - Target: >18 months (fundraising buffer)
    - Fréquence: Weekly calculation
    - Action: <12 months = fundraising activation
    
  Cash Efficiency:
    - Définition: Revenue generated per dollar burned
    - Mesure: Revenue growth / Cash burned
    - Target: >2x (efficient growth)
    - Fréquence: Monthly analysis
    - Action: <1.5x = efficiency improvement
```

## 6. ALERTING & ACTION TRIGGERS

### 6.1 Critical Alerts
```yaml
Red Alerts (Immediate Action):
  - System downtime >5 minutes
  - Customer churn >10% monthly
  - Burn rate >150% budget
  - Security breach detected
  
Yellow Alerts (24h Action):
  - Performance degradation >20%
  - NPS drop >10 points
  - Sales pipeline <3x target
  - Key talent resignation
  
Green Alerts (Weekly Review):
  - Feature adoption <50%
  - Support response >4h
  - Code coverage <80%
  - Marketing ROI <3x
```

### 6.2 Automated Actions
```yaml
Auto-Scaling Triggers:
  - CPU >80% = scale up

Business Triggers:
```
